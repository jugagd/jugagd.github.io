About JUGA GD


Around 2013 a small startup was born in Argentina, called "JUGA GD".

"JUGA" is "PLAY" in spanish. And what about "GD"? It stands for "Garage Developments" because that's where we started at!

We are a small company, but we dream BIG!

We focus on three fronts. Game Development, Front-End Web Development and Hardware Electronics Development.

With these three We aim to be able to help other fellow developers fulfill their dreams and also release our own crazy experiments!

Juan Manuel Garcia
Founder & Full Time Developer

MISSION: Our mission is to help everyone and inspire everyone to create awesome content. We know how frustrating it is to never get to bring forth your idea, and we are here to help you get there!


VISION: "Bring inspiration and innovation to every developer in the world."
